Gothic Masquerade Ball

The Black Rose Ball is a gothic masquerade ball in aid of charity. The ball will again return to its new home, the chapel in the Victorian cemetery in York.

This chapel is a rare design for the late Victorian period, inspired by the Greek Revival style based on the temple of Erectheus in Athens, and built in white stone from Roche Abbey in Yorkshire. Set in beautiful cemetery grounds, it is a wonderful venue for the ball.

Dance the night away to the best of Goth / Dark Wave / Dark Electro / Mittelalter, provided by DJs Victoria Fenbane (Slimelight, Shattenspiel, Exit the Grey), Rosenkavalier (Tanz Macabre) and Doktor Joy (Intrusion) in the chapel itself.

The Black Rose Bazaar will be in the visitor centre, near to the chapel. Doctor Geof will be on hand to draw portraits; and there will be a small selection of stalls for attendees to peruse (more details to be announced soon).

Bar

The ticket price includes one Black Rose cocktail on arrival. The well-stocked bar will be provided by The Yorkshire Tipsy Trailer; with a mobile bar just outside the chapel itself.

Ghost Tours

Rain and Uneven Ground

Please note that the event takes place in two buildings, the chapel and the visitor centre, which are a short open air walk away from each other. If it rains, please bring an umbrella.

Please also note that the cemetery grounds are often uneven in places - please ensure sensible footwear and only stick to marked pathways.
