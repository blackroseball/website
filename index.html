Gothic Masquerade Ball

The Black Rose Ball is an elegant gothic masquerade ball held in the chapel in the Victorian cemetery in York. Set in beautiful cemetery grounds, the chapel is a wonderful venue for the ball. Ball attendees will be free to enjoy the space all to themselves throughout the evening.

Full information about what is on offer at the ball can be found on the ticket page.

Dress Code and Arrival Times

The Black Rose Ball is a gothic masquerade ball, an elegant and extravagant event; a unique opportunity to wear the outfit of your dreams. The ideal place to wear the most impressive outfit you can dream up. Masks can be any format, be it the traditional mask, or lace, or veils, or even elaborate make up.

To ensure that all guests feel comfortable to get creative in elaborate attires, there is dress code for entry, which is formal / elegant / gothic / alternative. Although there is no strict policing, the right of admission is reserved if guests are not suitably attired.

Here is a moodboard for some inspiration - the ball is a wonderful opportunity to dress your best, and show off your style; whilst dancing in the chapel, or promenading along cemetery paths.

Guests need to arrive before 7pm, when the gates will be locked. There will be no tickets for sale on the door.

Age 18+ Right of Admission reserved.
